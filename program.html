What we do....

Education

EDUCATIONAL CAMPAIGNS

The main motive of YouWe is to promote sex education in every educational institution , sex education will be given on three levels. Primary, secondary and tertiary levels of sex education according to the age of the individual or group of individuals. Lack of sex education is main cause of rape . Whenever people hear the word 'sex' the general social taboo comes in our mind , or about sex education people just assume that we are talking about intercourse.Intercourse is a very mere part of sex education. Infact it is not all related to intercourse.

Awareness

AWARENESS CAMPAIGNS

The team of YouWe will and has conducted several awareness campaigns covering different topics which are needed to discuss.Breaking all the social taboos and assumptions , in form of nukad nataks and skits. YouWe has already conducted awareness camps on mensuration and rape. Telling about the norms and myth related to to mensuration and the breaking them. Lack of awareness is the one of the biggest reasons that we have such problems in the society. People lack knowledge and they build norms and taboos about things.

Webinars

WEBINARS

To reach the farthest places of our country we organise educational webinars for the people we can not reach physically . Distance can no more be a problem. YouWe will conduct several webinars regarding all the topics included in our mission. YouWe has already conducted several webinars regarding male rape , false rape allegations , sex education , marital rape , LGBTQIA and cyber security . And will continue to do so. By the availability of internet we can reach almost everyone and convey our message.

Clothes/Food distribution

CLOTHES/FOOD DISTRIBUTION

What could be better than helping a person in need . We help poor and needy people . We raise donation camps and collect clothes and food from the people willing to give . And then we distribute the clothes collected from donation camps to the needy people. We also go on collection drives and pick up the things from the donor’s place . YouWe has already conducted 5 cloth donation drives and 1 food distribution drive and has many such drives coming up to help the needy people of the city.

Covid support

COVID SUPPORT

COVID time wasn’t easy for anyone . We all went through a lot during those dark times. We all lost someone or something. In those hard times we the members of YouWe did what we could to help those who were in need . From food and basic necessities for the needy to medicals, and all possible help for the patients , YouWe did everything they could to help those who needed us intheir hard time.

Helpline

HELPLINE

Communication is the solution to all the problems. YouWe is creating a helpline, a secure place to build a bridge between the survivors and our people so we could listen to your problems and find the best solution. Many a times victims are not heard as their voice is suppressed , they are not allowed to speak for themselves and fight for justice. There are thousands of unregistered cases in India which are needed to come in light. We the people of YouWe will listen to everything happened with you and our team will strive to get you justice.
